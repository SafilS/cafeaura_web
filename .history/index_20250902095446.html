Refund & Cancellation Policy

Understanding our policies for order modifications, cancellations, and refunds

1. Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled under the following conditions:

  • Before Preparation: Full refund available if cancelled within 5 minutes of order placement
  • During Preparation: Partial refund (50%) if cancelled after 5 minutes but before completion
  • After Completion: No cancellation possible once order is ready for pickup

2. Refund Process

Refunds are processed as follows:

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Processing time: 3-5 business days for credit/debit cards
  • Digital wallet refunds: 24-48 hours
  • You will receive email confirmation when refund is processed

3. Quality Issues

If you receive food that doesn't meet quality standards:

  • Contact us immediately (within 30 minutes of pickup)
  • Provide photos if possible
  • We'll arrange for replacement or full refund
  • No questions asked for genuine quality concerns

4. Delivery Delays

For orders with significant delays:

  • Orders delayed by more than 15 minutes: 20% discount on next order
  • Orders delayed by more than 30 minutes: Full refund available
  • Automatic notifications for order status updates

5. Special Circumstances

We understand that emergencies happen:

  • Medical emergencies: Full refund with documentation
  • Campus closures: Full refund for affected orders
  • Technical issues: Compensation based on inconvenience caused
