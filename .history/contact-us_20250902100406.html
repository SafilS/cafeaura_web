Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 2-4 hours during business hours. For urgent technical issues, we aim to respond within 30 minutes.

Can I schedule a demo for my campus?

Absolutely! We'd love to show you how Cafeaura can transform your campus dining experience. Contact our business development team to schedule a personalized demo.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, UPI, digital wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay), and net banking. We're also working on adding cryptocurrency payments.

Do you provide training for cafeteria staff?

Yes! We provide comprehensive training for cafeteria staff and administrators to ensure smooth integration and operation of our platform.

How can I report a bug or technical issue?

You can report bugs through our support email, contact form, or directly through the app. Please include screenshots and detailed descriptions for faster resolution.

Do you offer custom solutions for specific campuses?

Yes, we understand that each campus has unique needs. Our team works closely with you to customize the platform according to your specific requirements and workflows.
