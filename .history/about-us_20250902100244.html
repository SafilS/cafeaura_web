The Cafeaura Story

It all started in 2023 when a group of engineering students at Tech Park Campus found themselves spending countless hours waiting in cafeteria queues. Between classes, assignments, and extracurricular activities, they realized that valuable time was being wasted on something as simple as getting a meal.

Frustrated by this daily inconvenience, they decided to create a solution. What began as a simple project to help their fellow students quickly evolved into something much bigger. They saw the potential to transform not just their campus, but campuses across the country.

Today, Cafeaura stands as a testament to student innovation and the power of solving real-world problems. Our platform has grown from serving a single cafeteria to managing multiple campus locations, helping thousands of students every day.
