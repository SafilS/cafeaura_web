1. Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled under the following conditions and timeframes:

Before Preparation (0-5 minutes): Full refund available if cancelled within 5 minutes of order placement

During Preparation (5+ minutes): Partial refund (50%) if cancelled after 5 minutes but before completion

After Completion: No cancellation possible once order is ready for pickup

Important Notes:

  • Cancellation requests must be made through the app or by contacting our support team
  • Time is calculated from the moment the order is confirmed
  • Orders that have already started preparation cannot be fully cancelled
  • Bulk orders may have different cancellation policies

2. Refund Process

Refunds are processed according to the following guidelines:

  • Processing Time: 3-5 business days for credit/debit cards
  • Digital Wallets: 24-48 hours for UPI, Paytm, PhonePe, etc.
  • Net Banking: 2-3 business days
  • Cash Refunds: Available at campus cafeterias within 24 hours

Refund Confirmation: You will receive an email confirmation when your refund is processed, including the refund amount and method.

Refund Eligibility:

  • Orders cancelled within the specified timeframes
  • Orders with quality issues reported within 30 minutes
  • Orders affected by system errors or technical issues
  • Orders cancelled due to campus closures or emergencies

3. Quality Issues

If you receive food that doesn't meet quality standards:

Immediate Action Required: Contact us immediately (within 30 minutes of pickup) to report quality issues

  • Documentation: Provide photos if possible for faster resolution
  • Replacement: We'll arrange for replacement or full refund
  • No Questions Asked: Genuine quality concerns are addressed without additional verification
  • Compensation: Additional compensation may be offered for significant issues

Common Quality Issues Covered:

  • Food that is cold when it should be hot
  • Food that is spoiled or has an off taste
  • Incorrect items or missing components
  • Food that appears to be undercooked or unsafe
  • Packaging issues that affect food quality

4. Delivery Delays

For orders with significant delays, we offer the following compensation:

15+ Minutes Delay: 20% discount on your next order

30+ Minutes Delay: Full refund available upon request

  • Automatic Notifications: You'll receive real-time updates on order status
  • Delay Compensation: Automatically applied to your account
  • Communication: Our team will proactively inform you of any delays
  • Alternative Options: We may offer alternative menu items if your original choice is unavailable

Delay Categories:

  • Minor Delay: 5-15 minutes (no compensation)
  • Moderate Delay: 15-30 minutes (20% discount on next order)
  • Significant Delay: 30+ minutes (full refund available)

5. Special Circumstances

We understand that emergencies and special situations can arise:

Medical Emergencies: Full refund with appropriate documentation (medical certificate, hospital admission, etc.)

Campus Closures: Full refund for all affected orders due to unexpected campus closures

Technical Issues: Compensation based on the inconvenience caused by system failures

  • Family Emergencies: Case-by-case evaluation with appropriate documentation
  • Weather Conditions: Full refund for orders affected by severe weather
  • Power Outages: Automatic refunds for orders during campus power failures
  • Staff Shortages: Proactive communication and compensation for service disruptions

Documentation Required: For special circumstances, please provide appropriate documentation to support your refund request.

6. Refund Methods

Refunds are processed through the original payment method used for the order:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Refunded to the same card within 3-5 business days
  • UPI Payments: Refunded to the same UPI ID within 24-48 hours
  • Digital Wallets: Refunded to Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay accounts within 24-48 hours
  • Net Banking: Refunded to the same bank account within 2-3 business days
  • Cash Payments: Cash refund available at campus cafeterias within 24 hours

Important: Refunds are always processed to the original payment method for security and compliance reasons.

Refund Processing Steps:

  • Refund request is received and validated
  • Order status is verified
  • Refund amount is calculated
  • Refund is processed through payment gateway
  • Confirmation email is sent to customer

7. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund decision or have concerns about our policy:

  • First Level: Contact our customer support team for immediate resolution
  • Escalation: Request escalation to a senior support representative
  • Management Review: Complex cases are reviewed by management
  • Documentation: Provide all relevant information and evidence
  • Timeline: Most disputes are resolved within 48-72 hours

Appeal Process: If you're still not satisfied, you may appeal the decision through our formal appeal process.

Appeal Process:

  • Submit a formal appeal within 7 days of the initial decision
  • Include all relevant documentation and evidence
  • Appeal is reviewed by a senior management team
  • Final decision is communicated within 5 business days
  • Decision is binding and final
